# Market Expansion Revenue Predictor

A regression-based market expansion dashboard built to estimate revenue potential across European markets.

This project combines search intelligence, competition analysis, economic indicators, and AI discoverability signals to support international market prioritisation.

## Overview

This model helps answer a strategic question:

**Which market offers the strongest commercial upside for expansion?**

The dashboard allows users to simulate:

- Budget allocation
- Estimated clicks
- Estimated conversions
- Revenue forecasts
- CPA
- ROAS
- Profitability

## Methodology

The model combines:

- Search demand
- CPC benchmarks
- Competition intensity
- Purchase intent signals
- Market growth trends
- GDP per capita
- Ecommerce maturity
- AI discoverability

A Linear Regression model is used to estimate revenue potential and support expansion decisions.
